Our Mission

Our purpose:

Renewing Hope, Rebuilding Lives

Our mission: Supplying people with the power (resources) to raise themselves up (self-advocacy) while renewing hope and opportunity (mutual aid). Our main goal is to create a resource hub for residents of the Greater Spokane Valley and work with city, county and nonprofit partners in ensuring folks are being directed to the appropriate organization. We believe when people are provided the support and tools to self-advocate, they gain a stronger voice, which leads to positive changes in their lives. We help folks learn how to problem-solve and speak up for themselves.
